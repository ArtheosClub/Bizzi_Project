# 1. Purpose

Prompt Governance defines how prompts are designed, approved, versioned, tested, deployed, monitored, controlled, and audited across the Art of Business AI ecosystem.

---

# 2. Mission

Ensure that prompts remain reliable, safe, traceable, reusable, explainable, and aligned with enterprise policies and business objectives.

---

# 3. Architectural Position

```text

↓
```

# 4. Core Principle

No production prompt may be executed without ownership, version control, approval, and auditability.
