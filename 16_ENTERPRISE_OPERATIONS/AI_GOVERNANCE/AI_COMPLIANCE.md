# 1. Purpose

AI Compliance defines how AI capabilities comply with enterprise policies, regulatory obligations, contractual commitments, governance requirements, audit expectations, and operational controls.

---

# 2. Mission

Ensure that all AI-enabled activities remain compliant, accountable, auditable, explainable, and aligned with enterprise governance standards.

---
