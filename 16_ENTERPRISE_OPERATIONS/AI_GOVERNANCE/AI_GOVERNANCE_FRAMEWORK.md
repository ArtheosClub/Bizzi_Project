# 1. Purpose

AI Governance Framework defines how artificial intelligence systems, agents, models, prompts, decisions, data use, risk controls, compliance obligations, and human oversight are governed inside the Art of Business enterprise operating model.

---

# 2. Mission

Ensure that AI-enabled enterprise execution remains accountable, safe, explainable, auditable, compliant, and aligned with business objectives.
