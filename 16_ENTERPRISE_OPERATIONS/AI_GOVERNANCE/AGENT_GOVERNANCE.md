# 1. Purpose

Agent Governance defines how AI agents are created, identified, authorized, supervised, monitored, controlled, audited, and retired across the Art of Business enterprise architecture.

---

# 2. Mission

Ensure that every agent operates within approved authority boundaries, follows governance policies, remains auditable, and contributes safely to enterprise outcomes.
