# 1. Purpose

Portfolio Management defines how enterprise initiatives, programs, projects, strategic investments, transformation efforts, and improvement portfolios are proposed, evaluated, prioritized, funded, executed, monitored, governed, and audited.

---

# 2. Mission

Ensure that enterprise resources are allocated to the highest-value work, aligned with strategy, constrained by capacity, governed by risk, and measured by outcomes.

---
