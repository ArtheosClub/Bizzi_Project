# 1. Purpose

Continuous Improvement defines how the enterprise identifies, evaluates, prioritizes, implements, validates, and institutionalizes improvements across business operations, services, workflows, governance, AI systems, and organizational capabilities.

---

# 2. Mission

Create a self-improving enterprise that continuously learns from execution, measurement, audits, incidents, customer feedback, and AI insights.
