# 1. Purpose

Partner Network defines how the enterprise discovers, evaluates, onboards, governs, develops, measures, and evolves strategic and operational partnerships within the Enterprise Ecosystem.

---

# 2. Mission

Enable the enterprise to build a trusted, scalable, resilient, and value-generating network of partners that expands capabilities, accelerates growth, reduces risk, and improves ecosystem outcomes.
