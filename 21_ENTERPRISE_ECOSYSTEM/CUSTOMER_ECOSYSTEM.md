# 1. Purpose

Customer Ecosystem defines how customers, customer organizations, communities, feedback loops, co-creation relationships, customer intelligence, and customer value networks are governed and integrated into the Enterprise Ecosystem.

---

# 2. Mission

Enable the enterprise to treat customers not only as buyers, but as active ecosystem participants who influence products, services, strategy, innovation, quality, and long-term value creation.
