# 1. Purpose

Ecosystem Intelligence defines how the enterprise ecosystem collects, integrates, analyzes, interprets, and distributes intelligence across partners, suppliers, customers, markets, risks, opportunities, and multi-enterprise operations.

---

# 2. Mission

Enable the ecosystem to make better collective decisions through shared, governed, timely, contextual, and actionable intelligence.
