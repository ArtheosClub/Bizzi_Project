# 1. Purpose

Ecosystem Risk Management defines how risks are identified, assessed, monitored, mitigated, escalated, and governed across the entire enterprise ecosystem, including partners, suppliers, customers, platforms, and multi-enterprise operations.

---

# 2. Mission

Protect ecosystem stability, resilience, trust, continuity, and value creation by proactively managing risks that emerge across interconnected enterprises.
