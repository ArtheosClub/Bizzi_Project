# 1. Purpose

Ecosystem Architecture defines how Art of Business extends beyond a single enterprise into a governed network of partners, suppliers, customers, platforms, regulators, services, and AI-orchestrated enterprises.

---

# 2. Mission

Enable multiple enterprises and ecosystem participants to coordinate, exchange value, share intelligence, manage risk, and evolve together through governed ecosystem orchestration.
