# 1. Purpose

Supplier Network defines how the enterprise discovers, qualifies, governs, monitors, optimizes, and evolves its supplier ecosystem to ensure resilience, continuity, performance, compliance, and value creation.

---

# 2. Mission

Enable the enterprise ecosystem to maintain a reliable, scalable, risk-aware, and strategically aligned supplier network capable of supporting long-term business objectives.
