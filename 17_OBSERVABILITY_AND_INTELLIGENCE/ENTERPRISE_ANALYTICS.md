# 1. Purpose

Enterprise Analytics defines how Art of Business transforms metrics, events, audit records, operational signals, business data, and AI-generated observations into enterprise insights, forecasts, recommendations, and decision intelligence.

---

# 2. Mission

Enable the enterprise to understand performance, detect patterns, anticipate risks, discover opportunities, and support better management decisions through trusted analytics.
