# 1. Purpose

Observability Architecture defines how the enterprise measures, monitors, analyzes, explains, audits, and learns from all operational activity occurring across the Art of Business ecosystem.

---

# 2. Mission

Create a fully observable enterprise where every process, workflow, service, agent, decision, event, and business outcome can be measured and understood.

---

# 4. Core Principle

Nothing important should happen without being observable.
