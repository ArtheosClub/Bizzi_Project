# 1. Purpose

Audit Intelligence defines how enterprise audit data is collected, correlated, reconstructed, analyzed, investigated, and transformed into actionable governance intelligence.

---

# 2. Mission

Provide complete transparency, accountability, traceability, and explainability for every significant enterprise action, decision, workflow, and AI activity.
