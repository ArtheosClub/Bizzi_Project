## 01. Purpose

AI-01 resolves constitutional interpretation questions identified during
the Governance Milestone Review (GMR-01), without amending Decision 0001,
DECISION_0002, ABR-01, or EGC-01. It creates no new constitutional
authority, changes no constitutional hierarchy, and introduces no new
governance process. It states how the existing text of those documents
is to be read where GMR-01 found the text unclear.

---

## 02. Interpretation 1 — Decision 0001 and Later Constitutional Documents

Decision 0001 established the governing constitutional principle: MVP
First.

ABR-01 and EGC-01 are interpreted as the minimum constitutional framework
necessary to enable stable MVP implementation. Their adoption is not a
departure from MVP First. They define the governance required to
implement the MVP safely and consistently, not an expansion of
architectural or governance ambition beyond that purpose.

---

## 03. Interpretation 2 — Architecture Freeze Timing

Decision 0001 established the Architecture Freeze immediately upon its
own approval.

ABR-01 did not create the Architecture Freeze. ABR-01 formalized:

- its operational governance;
- its constitutional status;
- its implementation procedures;

and confirmed its continued applicability.

No constitutional conflict exists regarding the Architecture Freeze.

---

## 04. Interpretation 3 — Authority Terminology

Within the current constitutional framework, references to "Chief
Architect" in Decision 0001 are interpreted as referring to the
constitutional authority presently exercised by the Project Owner,
unless a future constitutional document explicitly establishes the two
as distinct authorities. No redistribution of authority occurs.

---
