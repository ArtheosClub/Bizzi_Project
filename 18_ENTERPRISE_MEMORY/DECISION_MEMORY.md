# 1. Purpose

Decision Memory defines how enterprise decisions are captured, stored, contextualized, governed, retrieved, evaluated, and reused across the Art of Business operating model.

---

# 2. Mission

Ensure that the enterprise remembers what decisions were made, why they were made, who approved them, what alternatives were considered, and what outcomes resulted.

---
