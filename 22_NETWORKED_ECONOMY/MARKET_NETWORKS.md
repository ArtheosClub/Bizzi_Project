# 1. Purpose

Market Networks defines how interconnected markets are modeled, monitored, orchestrated, and optimized within the Networked Economy, enabling coordinated value exchange across enterprises, ecosystems, platforms, industries, and economic participants.

---

# 2. Mission

Provide a canonical architecture for understanding and governing market structures, market interactions, demand and supply dynamics, competitive forces, and value flows across interconnected economic networks.
