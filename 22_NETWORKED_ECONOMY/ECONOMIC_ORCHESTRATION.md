# 1. Purpose

Economic Orchestration defines how economic actors, enterprises, ecosystems, platforms, markets, value networks, and AI agents coordinate decisions, workflows, transactions, and resource allocation across the Networked Economy.

---

# 2. Mission

Provide a canonical orchestration architecture that enables autonomous yet governed coordination of economic activities, maximizing network-wide value while preserving resilience, transparency, accountability, and strategic alignment.
