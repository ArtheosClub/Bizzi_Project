# 1. Purpose

Networked Economy Architecture defines how Art of Business extends from enterprise ecosystems into interconnected economic networks composed of markets, platforms, value networks, industries, ecosystems, regulatory environments, and AI-orchestrated economic actors.

---

# 2. Mission
