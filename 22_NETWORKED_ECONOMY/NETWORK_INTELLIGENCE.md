# 1. Purpose

Network Intelligence defines the architecture for collecting, correlating, analyzing, predicting, and distributing intelligence across interconnected markets, platforms, enterprises, ecosystems, and value networks.

---

# 2. Mission

Transform distributed economic signals into coordinated, actionable intelligence that improves strategic decisions, operational execution, resilience, and value creation throughout the Networked Economy.
