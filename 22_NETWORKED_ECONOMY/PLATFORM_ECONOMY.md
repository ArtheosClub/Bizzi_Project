# 1. Purpose

Platform Economy defines the architecture, governance, operating model, and value creation mechanisms of digital platforms that connect participants across the Networked Economy.

---

# 2. Mission

Enable enterprises, ecosystems, customers, suppliers, partners, developers, and service providers to collaborate through scalable digital platforms with governed interactions, trusted transactions, and continuous innovation.
