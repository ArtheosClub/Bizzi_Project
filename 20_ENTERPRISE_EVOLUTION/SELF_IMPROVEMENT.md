# 1. Purpose

Self-Improvement defines how the enterprise systematically converts learning, metrics, audit findings, experience, and optimization signals into validated, standardized, and reusable improvements across the operating system.

---

# 2. Mission

Enable the enterprise to continuously improve its performance, quality, resilience, governance, decision-making, processes, agents, workflows, and strategic outcomes through governed improvement cycles.
