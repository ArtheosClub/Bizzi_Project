# 1. Purpose

Orchestra Evolution defines how the AI-orchestrated enterprise changes, restructures, expands, simplifies, and improves its agent organization, coordination models, authority paths, escalation routes, and decision networks over time.

---

# 2. Mission

Enable Art of Business to evolve the structure of its digital organization safely, deliberately, and continuously while preserving governance, accountability, operational continuity, and strategic alignment.

Autonomous orchestra evolution is allowed only for low-risk, reversible, pre-approved reconfigurations.

- temporary backup assignment;
- low-risk escalation rerouting;
- non-structural coordination tuning.
