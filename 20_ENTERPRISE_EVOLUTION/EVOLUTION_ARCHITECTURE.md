# 1. Purpose

Evolution Architecture defines how Art of Business continuously adapts, improves, restructures, and evolves its capabilities, processes, agents, workflows, governance, strategy, and operating model over time.

---

# 2. Mission

Enable the enterprise to become an adaptive autonomous organization that can learn from execution, respond to change, improve itself, and evolve under governed control.
