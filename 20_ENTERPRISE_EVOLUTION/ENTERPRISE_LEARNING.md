# 1. Purpose

Enterprise Learning defines how the enterprise transforms experience, observations, decisions, successes, failures, and operational outcomes into reusable organizational knowledge that improves future behavior.

---

# 2. Mission

Enable the enterprise to continuously learn from execution and convert learning into measurable improvements across agents, processes, governance, strategy, and operations.
