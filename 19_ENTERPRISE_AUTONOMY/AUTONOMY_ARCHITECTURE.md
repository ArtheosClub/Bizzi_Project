# 1. Purpose

Autonomy Architecture defines how Art of Business enables controlled enterprise autonomy across agents, workflows, decisions, operations, planning, optimization, recovery, and governance.

---

# 2. Mission

Create a governed autonomy layer that allows the enterprise to act with increasing independence while remaining aligned with strategy, authority, risk controls, compliance obligations, and human oversight requirements.
