# 1. Purpose

Autonomous Execution defines how enterprise agents, workflows, services, and orchestration components execute approved business activities independently within governed autonomy boundaries.

---

# 2. Mission

Enable the enterprise to transform approved plans, decisions, and objectives into autonomous actions while maintaining accountability, observability, compliance, security, and auditability.
