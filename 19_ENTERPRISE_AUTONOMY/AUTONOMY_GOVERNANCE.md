# 1. Purpose

Autonomy Governance defines the policies, controls, authority structures, oversight mechanisms, approval models, audit requirements, and risk boundaries governing autonomous enterprise behavior.

---

# 2. Mission

Ensure that enterprise autonomy remains aligned with strategy, governance, risk appetite, compliance obligations, human accountability, and organizational objectives.
