# 1. Purpose

Self-Planning defines how the enterprise autonomously creates, evaluates, updates, coordinates, and optimizes plans across strategic, tactical, operational, and execution horizons while remaining aligned with governance, risk, capacity, and business objectives.

---

# 2. Mission

Enable the enterprise to continuously transform goals into executable plans with minimal human intervention while maintaining control, traceability, and accountability.
