# 1. Purpose

Self-Healing defines how the enterprise autonomously detects, diagnoses, contains, recovers from, validates, and learns from operational disruptions, failures, incidents, workflow breakdowns, and service degradation.

---

# 2. Mission

Enable the enterprise to restore normal operations with minimal human intervention while maintaining governance, auditability, safety, and business continuity.
