# 1. Purpose

Self-Optimization defines how the enterprise autonomously identifies, evaluates, prioritizes, executes, validates, and learns from optimization opportunities across processes, workflows, agents, services, resources, risks, and business outcomes.

---

# 2. Mission

Enable the enterprise to continuously improve performance, efficiency, quality, resilience, and strategic alignment through governed autonomous optimization.
