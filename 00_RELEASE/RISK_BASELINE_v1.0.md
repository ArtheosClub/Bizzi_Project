# 1. Purpose

This document defines the official risk baseline for Art of Business v1.0.

It establishes the minimum risk-management structure required to operate an AI-orchestrated enterprise architecture across enterprise operations, agents, tools, data, memory, autonomy, evolution, ecosystems, networked economies and global intelligent economic coordination.

# 18. Risk Escalation Baseline

Escalation is required when:

- risk exceeds threshold;
- authority boundary is unclear;
- impact is high;
- residual risk is unacceptable;
- compliance obligation is affected;
- customer or ecosystem trust is affected;
- autonomous action may become irreversible;
- systemic exposure is detected.

---

# 19. Risk Acceptance Baseline

Risk acceptance requires:

- identified owner;
- documented rationale;
- impact assessment;
- mitigation review;
- residual risk statement;
- approval authority;
- expiration or review date;
- audit reference.
