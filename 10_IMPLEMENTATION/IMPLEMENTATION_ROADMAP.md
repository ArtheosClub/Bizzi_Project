# 1. Purpose

The Implementation Roadmap defines the phased evolution of Art of Business from architecture specification to a fully operational AI-Orchestrated Enterprise Platform.

It transforms:

```text
Vision
→ Architecture
→ Platform
→ Enterprise Operations
→ Autonomous Enterprise
```

---

# 2. Strategic Objective

Create a reusable enterprise operating platform capable of managing small and medium businesses through a governed network of AI agents.

Target outcomes:

- reduced operational dependency on individuals;
- scalable enterprise knowledge;
- accelerated decision making;
- automation of management functions;
- creation of a Digital Twin Enterprise.

---
